Global Warming Awareness

Global warming is a long-term rise in Earth's temperature, primarily caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial emissions.

It leads to climate change, rising sea levels, and extreme weather events like wildfires and hurricanes.

Reducing carbon footprints, using renewable energy, and planting trees are some ways to combat global warming. Every small action counts in preserving our planet for future generations.

Switching to renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power can significantly reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Recycling and reducing waste can also help mitigate climate change by lowering pollution and conserving resources.
